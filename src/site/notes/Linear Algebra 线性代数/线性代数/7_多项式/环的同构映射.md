如果环$R$到环$P^{\prime}$映射$\sigma$是**双射**，且满足
$$
\begin{align}
\sigma(\alpha + \beta) = \sigma(\alpha) + \sigma(\beta) \\
\sigma(k\alpha) = k\sigma(\alpha)
\end{align}

$$
称$\sigma$是环$R$到$R^{\prime}$的**同构映射**
